Project

IMAG brings investment management to life through a web-based simulation where learners act as portfolio managers. Participants practise diversification, risk‚Äìreturn decisions, and sustainable (ESG) investing in realistic scenarios, with modular complexity for schools, universities, and professional training. Guided onboarding, open teaching resources, and competition-ready formats make it easy to adopt in courses and workshops.
Our game, Cesim Invest, focuses on strategic fund management, ESG-integrated investing, and tactical decision-making. Participants manage diversified portfolios, integrate ESG factors, and oversee corporate strategies, gaining practical experience in asset management and investment decision-making.
